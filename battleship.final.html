Battleship

HOW TO PLAY: Size the sea to your preference and send forth as many hidden ships as you can successfully meet in battle. Then begin guess-shooting using either the aim-shoot form or your mouse cannon to click the cells you think may be hiding part of an enemy battleship. Note that you can resize the sea and/or send forth more ships mid-battle. If you choose to add another ship after you have already made a shot or two that missed, the miss-colored cells will be turned light blue again but your total miss count will remain unchanged. To stop the game and start over at any time, press the "Start Over" button. May ultimate victory over the sea be yours!

Fire when ready!
